Detailed Description

Functor for comparing symbols by dominant colors.

Other than compareByColorPriority(), this comparison uses the lowest priority which exists for a particular color in the map. All map colors are preprocessed in the constructor. Thus the functor becomes invalid as soon as colors are changed.
